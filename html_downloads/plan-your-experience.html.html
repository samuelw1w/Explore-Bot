Hotels & Transportation

VMware Explore Shuttle Service

Shuttle transportation has been arranged for VMware Explore attendees at the following times. Shuttles are available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Arrival Transfer Shuttles from Airport to Fira Gran Via

Sunday, 5 November: 15:00 - 18:00

Monday, 6 November: 10:00 - 18:00

 

  • Approximately every 30 minutes
  • Pick-up from Terminal 1 and Terminal 2
  • Meet at arrivals after customs. Look for staff with event branded signs/paddles who will direct attendees to the shuttles
  • Please note there is not a VMware Explore support/help desk at the airport
  • Drop off at event venue, Fira Gran Via, outside the Hall 8.0 entrance on Carrer de l’Alumini
  • Register and collect your VMware Explore badge, then proceed to hotel on own

The Party Shuttles from Fira Gran Via to Central Barcelona

Wednesday, 8 November: 19:30 - 21:45

 

  • Approximately every 30 minutes
  • Shuttles available to take you back to central Barcelona after The Party
  • Departs from the Fira Gran Via outside Hall 8.0 on Carrer de l’Alumini
  • Drop-off at two central locations
    • Plaça d’Espanya
      Nearby metro station: Espanya
    • Barceloneta (Passeig de Colom, 1)
      Nearby metro stations: Jaume 1 and Barceloneta 

Departure Transfer Shuttles from Fira Gran Via to Airport

Thursday, 9 November: 11:00 - 15:30

 

  • Approximately every 30 minutes
  • Departs from the Fira Gran Via outside Hall 8.0 on Carrer de l’Alumini
  • Drop-off at Terminal 1 and Terminal 2

Official Hotels

Below, you’ll find a list of hotels to choose from while attending VMware Explore Barcelona. These hotels have been chosen with convenience and budgetary requirements in mind to best fit your needs. To secure a room at the event rate, make your hotel arrangements via the VMware Explore booking tool after you complete registration. You are also free to make your own reservations directly with the hotel of your choice if you would like to book outside of the VMware Explore hotel block.

Official Evening Events

  1. Welcome Reception
  2. Hall Crawl
  3. The Party

Welcome Reception

When: 17:00 – 19:00 on Tuesday, 7 November
Where: The Expo, Hall 7.0

Kick off your VMware Explore experience at the welcome reception in The Expo. Get your first look at the show floor, network with fellow attendees in a fun atmosphere, pick up some new swag, and enjoy delicious appetizers and drinks.

Hall Crawl

When: 17:00 – 19:00 on Wednesday, 8 November
Where: The Expo, Hall 7.0

The hall crawl gives you the chance to get up close and personal with the VMware ecosystem, sponsors and exhibitors in The Expo. Participate in giveaways, get in-depth product information, pick up some swag, and socialize while enjoying snacks and drinks at the sponsor stands.

The Party

When: 19:00 – 21:00 on Wednesday, 8 November
Where: Hall 4.0

Work hard, play hard. The Party is our must-attend event. Plan to enjoy some food, drinks, games and, of course, great music from Manic Street Preachers.
